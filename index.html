Eleanor Wilder (real name Nora Roberts), with many more than one hundred published novels, is one of the most important writers in the romance genre. She was the first writer to cross the Romance Writers of America's Hall of Fame and knows how to combine love with intrigue and mystery to perfection in her books. She currently resides with her husband in Maryland.

Pseudonyms: Her real name is Eleanor Wilder. Her pseudonyms are Nora Roberts, Jill March, Sarah Hardesty and J.D. Robb.

‘I love my work, even on a bad day, I love my work. Being a writer is a gift I’m grateful for, even when it’s a bad day.’
