Welcome to our world of hospitality!


With the warmth and welcome of a Bed and Breakfast combined with the luxury of a boutique hotel, Inn BoonsBoro offers you an unforgettable experience.



The inn is owned by no other than Maryland resident and New York Times best selling author, Nora Roberts. You may have also read her work under the pseudonym J.D. Robb, from the in Death series.
Old records suggest that the building dates back to the 1790s. After being abandoned for some time, Nora Roberts purchased and renovated the structure. It wasn't easy getting to opening day, as a fire tragically burnt most of the building. After much work from local contractors, Inn Boonsboro finally opened in February 2009.
One of the most intriguing details of this boutique hotel is that it's literary-themed. All rooms are based on the love story and era from iconic storybook characters.
There are 8 rooms total, all unique in their own way.




Featured Rooms

Inn BoonsBoro guests enjoy a delicious 2-course breakfast served by our staff each morning of your stay. A complimentary wine & charcuterie is offered to guests from 6:00 to 7:00 pm each evening. Bottled water, assorted soft drinks, and various snacks are also provided by your innkeeper throughout your stay.

All guest rooms have access to one of our charming porches, either directly from the room or steps away in the corridor. Guest rooms on the second and third floors balconies include a bistro table and two chairs on their porch. All guests have access to the Fit In BoonsBoro facility adjacent to our parking lot.
